Extended Kalman Filter with Corner-Based Landmark Localization 2024

For a differential drive mobile robot in Gazebo, wheel-encoder odometry is fused with LIDAR landmark (corner) detections using an Extended Kalman Filter (EKF). The EKF propagates motion and corrects pose with measurement updates from detected corners to provide continuous, drift-reduced localization.
